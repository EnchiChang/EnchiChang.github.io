书单

发表于2026-03-14|更新于2026-04-18|书单
|浏览量:

我想列出目前我能记起来的读过的外国文学。这些书构成了我生命，也是你了解我的一个非常重要的途径。我想先用书的原文列出，并且注上英文翻译，这样最忠实于书本身；再用中文列出，这样就很方便理解了。我并没有学过法语，但法国文学构成了我阅读生命最大的一部分。
I want to list all the foreign literature I can currently remember reading. These books have shaped my life and are also a very important way for you to understand me. I’d like to first list them in their original languages with English translations, which is the most faithful to the books themselves; then list them separately in Chinese, French, and Japanese, which will be easier to understand. I have never studied French, but French literature has formed the largest part of my reading life.

Books Read (Foreign Literature) (Novels, Novellas & Plays)

Elementary School: Le Petit Prince (The Little Prince), The Adventures of Tom Sawyer, The Adventures of Huckleberry Finn, The Old Man and the Sea, Cuore (Heart)

Middle School: A Tale of Two Cities, Преступление и наказание (Crime and Punishment), Madame Bovary, Le Rouge et le Noir (The Red and the Black), Jane Eyre, Die Leiden des jungen Werthers (The Sorrows of Young Werther), 伊豆の踊子 (The Dancing Girl of Izu), Er redete mit dem Vieh, den Vögeln und den Fischen (King Solomon’s Ring), Hamlet, Vingt mille lieues sous les mers (Twenty Thousand Leagues Under the Sea), The £1,000,000 Bank Note, 人間失格 (No Longer Human), The Great Gatsby, Nineteen Eighty-Four

High School: Война и мир (War and Peace), Le Père Goriot (Père Goriot), Wuthering Heights, Анна Каренина (Anna Karenina), La Dame aux camélias (The Lady of the Camellias), El amor en los tiempos del cólera (Love in the Time of Cholera), Cien años de soledad (One Hundred Years of Solitude), Moby-Dick, Il visconte dimezzato (The Cloven Viscount), Notre-Dame de Paris (The Hunchback of Notre-Dame), Nesnesitelná lehkost bytí (The Unbearable Lightness of Being), Miguel Street, Et dukkehjem (A Doll’s House), The Nightingale and the Rose, Salomé, La Peste (The Plague), L’Étranger (The Stranger), Lost Horizon, L’Amant (The Lover), Im Westen nichts Neues (All Quiet on the Western Front), Der Process (The Trial), Don Quijote de la Mancha (Don Quixote), The Moon and Sixpence, Der Steppenwolf (Steppenwolf), Dans le café de la jeunesse perdue (In the Café of Lost Youth), 羅生門 (Rashomon), Animal Farm, En attendant Godot (Waiting for Godot)

University: 雪国 (Snow Country), Nesmrtelnost (Immortality), Dimanches d’août (Sundays in August), Rue des Boutiques Obscures (Missing Person), Il cavaliere inesistente (The Nonexistent Knight), Il barone rampante (The Baron in the Trees), The Scarlet Letter, Тёмные аллеи (Dark Avenues), Jean-Christophe, To the Lighthouse, Das Schloss (The Castle), Carmen, Die Reise in die Vergangenheit (Journey into the Past), Brief einer Unbekannten (Letter from an Unknown Woman), Scharlach (Scarlet Fever), Schachnovelle (The Royal Game), El jardín de senderos que se bifurcan (The Garden of Forking Paths), Memoria de mis putas tristes (Memories of My Melancholy Whores), Doce cuentos peregrinos (Strange Pilgrims), Братья Карамазовы (The Brothers Karamazov), 眠れる美女 (The House of the Sleeping Beauties), 細雪 (The Makioka Sisters), Pedro Páramo, 古都 (The Old Capital), 千羽鶴 (Thousand Cranes), 山の音 (The Sound of the Mountain), こころ (Kokoro), Le Procès-verbal (The Interrogation), La Porte étroite (Strait Is the Gate)

书单（外国文学）（中长篇）

小学：小王子、汤姆索亚历险记、哈克贝利费恩历险记、老人与海、爱的教育

初中：双城记、罪与罚、包法利夫人、红与黑、简爱、少年维特的烦恼、伊豆的舞女、所罗门王的指环、哈姆雷特、海底两万里、百万英镑、人间失格、了不起的盖茨比、1984

高中：战争与和平、高老头、呼啸山庄、安娜卡列尼娜、茶花女、霍乱时期的爱情、百年孤独、白鲸、分成两半的子爵、巴黎圣母院、不能承受的生命之轻、米格尔街、玩偶之家、夜莺与玫瑰、莎乐美、鼠疫、局外人、消失的地平线、情人、西线无战事、审判、堂吉诃德、月亮与六便士、荒原狼、青春咖啡馆、罗生门、动物农场、等待戈多

大学：雪国、不朽、八月的星期天、暗店街、看不见的骑士、树上的男爵、红字、暗径集、约翰克里斯托夫、到灯塔去、城堡、卡门、昨日之旅、一个陌生女人的来信、猩红热、象棋的故事、小径分叉的花园、苦妓回忆录、梦中的欢快葬礼和十二个异乡故事、卡拉马佐夫兄弟、睡美人、细雪、佩德罗·巴拉莫、古都、千羽鹤、山音、心、诉讼笔录、窄门
